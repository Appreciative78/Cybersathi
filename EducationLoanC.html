Instructions for Filling Out the Scholarship Form

Before starting the form, carefully read through all sections to understand the required information. Ensure you have the necessary documents and details ready for a smooth application process.

Personal Information

Enter your full name exactly as it appears on your official documents. Ensure the provided date of birth is accurate and formatted correctly. Use the dropdown menu to select your gender.

Communication Details

Provide your nationality along with the city, state, and pincode for your area. Enter your complete address, ensuring clarity and correctness.

Format and Accuracy

All entered information must match your official records. Pay attention to capitalization and correct spelling, especially for names and addresses. Follow specific input field requirements, such as using only alphabets where required.

Mandatory Fields

Fields marked with asterisks (*) or highlighted as required must be completed. The form will not be accepted if any of these fields are left blank.

Submission

Review all the information before clicking the Submit button.Once submitted, changes may not be possible. Double-check for typos or errors.

Follow-Up

After submission, you may receive a confirmation email or notification. Keep it for your records.

Assistance

If you encounter difficulties, reach out to the scholarship office or designated contact person.

Short Notice for Students

If you are selected for the next round of the scholarship, you will be notified via email.
